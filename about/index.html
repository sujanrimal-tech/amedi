About Satya MediTech Solution

Our Story

Founded with a passion for combining healthcare support and technological innovation,Satya MediTech Solutions was established to bridge the gap between critical biomedical services and modern electrical and electronic needs. What started as a small technical service has grown into a comprehensive solutions provider, trusted by healthcare institutions, industries, and businesses for reliable expertise and long-term support. We are driven by a simple principle: “Real solutions. Real impact.”

Our Mission

To supply and service high-quality biomedical, electrical, and electronic equipment, ensuring our clients have reliable tools to achieve precision, safety, and efficiency in their work.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of integrated biomedical, electrical, and electronic solutions in Nepal and beyond, recognized for our technical excellence, trusted partnerships, and positive impact on society.
